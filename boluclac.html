BoLucLac Recipe

Description

A classic vietnamese-french dished that utilizes a combination of a soft tender meat along with french fries and a side of salad.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Vegetable
  • Potatoe
  • Onion
  • Bell Peppers
  • Rice

Steps

  • Put rice into the rice cooker
  • Chop the meat into cubes
  • Use Extra virgin olive oil and sear the meat for 1 minute each side
  • Slice onion and throw them in for 30 secs
  • Slice potatoes and put into fryer
  • Slice vegetables up
  • Plate everything together
