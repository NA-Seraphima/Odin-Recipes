Pad Thai Recipe

Description

Pad thai, xphat thai, or phad thai, is a stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food in Thailand as part of the country's cuisine. It is typically made with rice noodles, shrimp, peanuts, a scrambled egg, and bean sprouts, among other vegetables. The ingredients are fried in a wok.

Ingredients

  • rice noodles
  • chicken
  • shrimp
  • tofu
  • peanuts
  • pad thai sauce
  • scrambled eggs

    • Steps

    • Whisk together pad thai sauce
    • Prep protein and vegetables
    • Whisk the eggs
    • Cook the noodles
    • Stir fry it all together
