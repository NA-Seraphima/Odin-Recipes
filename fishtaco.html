Fish Taco Recipe

Description

Savory fish mixed with veggies and a lime for a delicious and healthy meal!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Fish of choice(salmon,cod,tilapia)
  • Coleslaw
  • Limes
  • Avocado
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  • Bake the tortilla shells to make them crisp
  • Cover the fish in batter
  • Put fish into fryer
  • Put fish into tortilla and add vegetables
  • Add avocado and chili flakes
  • sSqueeze lime and enjoy
